Shivam Duggal

Email / CV / Google Scholar / Github / Twitter

I am a master's student at Carnegie Mellon University's robotics institute (CMU MSR) advised by Prof. Deepak Pathak.

Previously, I worked as a Research Scientist at Uber ATG, Toronto, where I was advised by Prof. Raquel Urtasun. I also worked closely with Prof. Shenlong Wang. I obtained my Bachelor's in Computer Science from Delhi College of Engineering, India (DTU/DCE).

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision, graphics, and robotics. I am particularly interested in topics such as self-supervised learning, physics-based vision, 2D/3D scene understanding (perception, reconstruction and simulation), with applications relating to AR/ VR and robotics.
