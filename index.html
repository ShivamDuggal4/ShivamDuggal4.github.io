I am working as a Research Scientist at Uber ATG, Toronto, where I am advised by Professor Raquel Urtasun. I also work closely with Professor Shenlong Wang. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Delhi Technological Unviersity, India (DTU, formerly DCE) in August 2017.

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision, computer graphics and robotics. Currently, I am interested in developing algorithms/ systems which can enable efficient 3D scene understanding through 3D perception, 3D reconstruction, novel-view synthesis etc.
